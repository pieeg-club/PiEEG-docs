# How to Start

### MicroBCI. Shield to Measure EEG with NUCLEO-WB55 STMicroelectronics Development Boards & Kit. EEG with STM32.

To start measuring Bio Data with MicroBCI, it is necessary to have the following parts:

- Power Bank (not more than 10000mAh and output current – 3A)

- EEG cap

- 8 EEG electrodes (dry or wet) and cables

- 2 clips Electrodes

- Micro USB cable

Connect the MicroBCI shield to the Nucleo Board, and after that, connect the device to a battery (power supply) and connect the electrodes.

Pay attention to the right connection for MicroBCI and the Nucleo Board.

![Measure EEG with Nucleo Board](/images/hardware/2025/09/connect-300x188.png)

Full galvanic isolation from the main power supply is required during bio measurement. Total disconnection from any power supply is required, except a 5V battery.

To measure EEG, use an EEG cap kit with [dry electrodes](https://pieeg.com/product/dry-electrodes/), [wet electrodes](https://pieeg.com/product/eeg-electrodes/), or an [EMG kit](https://pieeg.com/product/emg-electrodes/).
