# Required Equipment

To start measuring Bio Data with PiEEG, it is necessary to have the following parts:

- Power Bank: A 5V power bank with a minimum output current of 3A and a capacity of no more than 10,000 mAh. See the Power Bank page for full requirements and recommendations.

- Peripherals: A USB keyboard and USB mouse to interact with the Raspberry Pi desktop.

- Display: A portable monitor or screen. Power it directly from the power bank using a USB cable.

- Raspberry Pi: RasberryPi4,5(with mciroSD card, connection to power bank)

- EEG cap: A cap to hold the electrodes in position on the scalp. See the Cap page for options including the recommended PiEEG nylon cap and DIY instructions.

- Electrodes: 16 EEG electrodes (dry or Ag/AgCl wet) with cables, plus 2 clip electrodes for the reference and bias connections. See the Electrodes page for compatible types.
