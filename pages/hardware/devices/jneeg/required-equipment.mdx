# What need equipment except JNEEG

To start measuring Bio Data with JNEEG, it is necessary to have the following parts:

- Power Bank (not more than 10000mAh and output current - 3A)

- Cabel Keyboard

- Cable PC Mouse

- Screen (conenction to power bank)

- Jetson Nano (conenction to power bank)

- EEG cap

- 8 EEG electrodes (dry or wet) and cables

- 2 clips Electrodes
