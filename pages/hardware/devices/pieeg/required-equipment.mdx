# What need equipment except PiEEG

To start measuring Bio Data with PiEEG, it is necessary have the next parts:

- Power Bank (not more than 10000mAh and output current at least 3A for JNEEG and PiEEG, not more 1000 mah and 5V for ardEEG, current not more 2 A)

- Cable Keyboard

- Cable PC Mouse

- Screen (conenction to power bank)

- RasberryPi4,5(with mciroSD card, connection to power bank)

- EEG cap

- 8 EEG electrodes (dry or wet) and cables

- 2 clips Eelctrodes
