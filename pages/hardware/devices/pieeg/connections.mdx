# How to start. Connections with PiEEG

Connect the shield to Raspberry Pi via GPIO40

Connect the electrodes and the clip electrodes, as shown in the picture.

![Image](https://pieeg.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Connection.bmp)

1 - 8 ch Electrodes located on the head to measure EEG

Reference Electorde (clip) in ear, to measurre EEG

Bias Electorde (clip) in ear to remove electromagnetic noise from the signal

Also, we have a manual for a quick start. The link is  [here](https://github.com/pieeg-club/PiEEG/blob/main/Manual.%20Conenction%20and%20start%20-%20PiEEG1.pdf)
