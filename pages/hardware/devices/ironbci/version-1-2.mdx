# How to start


### Required Equipment
For EEG measurements:
- LiPo battery — 200 mAh recommended, 2.0 mm JST connector
- EEG cap
- 8 EEG electrodes (dry or wet) with cables — 2.54 mm connector
- 2 clip electrodes (reference and bias)

**ironbci** - Low-cost Wearable BLE 8 channels for dry and wet electrodes, brain-computer interface with mobile SDK

![How connect electrodes to brain-computer interface](/images/hardware/2025/07/251308-3-2-300x125.jpg)

### Step 1 — Connect the Battery
The LiPo battery connector is located on the bottom side of the IronBCI board. Plug in the battery before powering on.

> [!WARNING]
> **Reverse Polarity Risk**: Some JST connectors have reversed polarity. Before connecting the battery for the first time, verify that the polarity of your battery matches the connector on the board. Connecting a reversed-polarity battery **will damage the device**.

Connector for the lippo battery located on the bottom side of the device.

![connect battery to ironbci device](/images/hardware/2025/07/irronbci_1.bmp)

**Warning!** Some JST connector has the wrong polarity. Before making a connection, confirm that the polarity of the battery is correct

![Image](/images/hardware/2025/07/New-Bitmap-image-9.png)

**2. Turn Switcher**

To start transmitting data need turn **ON** both switchers

During charging, Switch 1 - **ON**, Switch 2 - **OFF**. Dont use the device during Charging.

![Image](/images/hardware/2025/07/ironbci_2.bmp)

**3. Charging Status LED**

For charging, use the Micro USB cable 5V

LED 1 is **ON** during charging

LED 2 if **ON** - Battery was charged

![Image](/images/hardware/2025/07/irronbci_133.bmp)

**4. BLE statys LED**

If the connection is successful between the ironbci and Laptop or mobile Phone Status BLE LED is **ON**

![Image](/images/hardware/2025/07/itronbci_4-3.bmp)

Once both switches are turned **ON**, the device starts publishing advertisements via BLE. Once connected to the device, the device starts transmitting data via 8 channels at a frequency of 250 samples per second.

To start work with ironbci, you need a Lippo Battery (recommend **200 mAh**). Connector with a wire 2.0mm JST Connector

To measure the EEG

- EEG cap

- 8 EEG electrodes (dry or wet) and cables (connector 2.54)

- 2 clips Electrodes

To measure EMG, EKG, and EOG

- EMG electrodes

- cables (connector 2.54)
