###  Before You Begin
>Safety — Battery Power Only. IronBCI-32 must be powered from a 5V USB battery only. The device must never be connected to mains power via USB or any other means. To ensure galvanic isolation and eliminate electromagnetic interference, the laptop receiving data must also run on battery power only — physically disconnected from the AC mains supply.   



**Connect Power and Serial Data**

Needs two cables: USB and Micro USB 

![Image](/images/hardware/2026/01/ST-Link-Connection-1.png)

Fig.1.1  Micro USB cable in both cases

One Micro Cable for Power Supply  - Power  

One Micro USB  Cable for Stream Data via Serial Port 

**Attention - The data streamer cable should be a cable that can transmit data in Fig.1.2 (not just for power, 4 cables inside)**

![Image](/images/hardware/2026/01/Stream-data.png)

       Fig.1.2.  Connection - Power USB and Stream cables

**To ensure galvanic isolation and minimize electromagnetic interference (EMI), the IronBCI-32 must transmit data to a laptop operating solely on DC battery power.

The laptop must be physically disconnected from the AC mains power supply to eliminate 50/60Hz line noise and prevent ground loop interference .**

>The device must operate only from a battery - 5 V. Complete isolation from the mains power is required! The device MUST not be connected to any kind of mains power, via USB or otherwise.

Power supply - only battery 5V.
