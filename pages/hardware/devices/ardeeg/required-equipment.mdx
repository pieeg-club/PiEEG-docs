# What need equipment except ardEEG

To start measuring Bio Data with ardEEG, it is necessary to have the following parts:

- Power Bank (not more than 300 mah and 5V, current not more than 2A) for Arduino

- Arduino UNO R4

- EEG cap

- 8 EEG electrodes (dry or wet) and cables

- 2 clips Electrodes
