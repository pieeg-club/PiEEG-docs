# Power Bank

All devices must work only from a battery 5V, with power not more than 10000mAh and an output current of at least 3A for JNEEG and piEEG, but not more than 3.5 A, with 5V and not more than 1000mAh for ardEEg. Can be used as one power bank to supply monitors for all PiEEG devices (PiEEG, ArdEEG, JNEEG...) USB cable for power supply should short as possible and **sufficient thickness**
