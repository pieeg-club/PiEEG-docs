# Electrodes

### To measure EEG signals with PiEEG devices, electrodes are required. PiEEG is compatible with various types of electrodes that use a 2.54 mm pin pitch connector. In our typical setups, we use dry electrodes or Ag/AgCl (silver/silver chloride) electrodes, but other compatible types can also be used depending on the application.
